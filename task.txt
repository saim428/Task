Task
Create a class hierarchy representing different types of vehicles. 
The hierarchy should include at least three classes: Vehicle, Car, and Motorcycle.
Vehicle class:
Properties: make, model, year, color
Methods: start(), stop(), getDetails()
Car class:
Inherits from Vehicle
Additional properties: numberOfDoors, engineType
Additional methods: openDoor(), closeDoor()
Motorcycle class:
Inherits from Vehicle
Additional properties: engineType, hasSidecar
Additional methods: startEngine(), stopEngine()
Requirements:
Demonstrate inheritance and polymorphism.
Use appropriate data types and access modifiers.
Implement methods to showcase object behavior.
Consider potential enhancements or additional features.
